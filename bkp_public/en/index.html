Essences

I want to share my poetry with a world that doesn't take shape through its people. This space is not a showcase, it is a shelter.

A shelter for a voice that longed deeply to be heard, ashamed of such a narcissistic desire. Here, then, are archived the words that slipped from my soul — the poems, the philosophies, the essences.

Perhaps it will become, sooner or later, an archive of posthumous memories. But may it serve, while it lasts, as an offering to reflection, to the rescue of feeling, to poetry in its rawest form — born without ornament, without the intention to please, only coming from the soul.
