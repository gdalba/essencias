A warm bath.<br>
A gentle breeze.<br>
The smile of a newborn.<br>
The laughter of the elder.<br>

Cherry blossoms in spring.<br>
Grandma’s bread, fresh from the oven.<br>
A family full of hope, fresh off the boat.<br>
Apples just torn from the tree.<br>
Honey, stolen straight from the bees.<br>

Stardew over morning leaves.<br>
Gentle rain licking the window.<br>
Sunset shades licking the mountain.<br>
A sunflower garden.<br>
A mother’s embrace.<br>
A Sunday prayer.<br>

The weep of a violin.<br>
A soprano’s lament.<br>

Sara’s name.<br>

The spark of a first kiss.<br>
The spark of Sara’s kiss.<br>

Sara’s embrace.<br>
Sara’s hug.<br>
Sara’s voice.<br>
Sara’s cheeks.<br>
Sara’s eyes.<br>
Sara’s eyebrows.<br>
Sara’s ears.<br>
Sara’s mind.<br>
Sara’s arms.<br>
Sara’s legs.<br>

Sara’s gardens.<br>
Sara’s rivers.<br>
Sara’s prayers.<br>
Sara’s dreams.<br>
All the dreams.<br>

A million stars colliding.<br>
The nuclear flowers of Antares.<br>
The strawberry rains of universe seventy-four.<br>

A million Saras destined to love me.<br>
A million Saras in sorrow.<br>

Sara’s sorrow.<br>
Sara’s hopes.<br>

A million hopes<br>
for a million people<br>
to be sacrificed<br>
for Sara’s one hope.
